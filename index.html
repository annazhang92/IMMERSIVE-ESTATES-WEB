Meet Immersive Estates


  • Our Story

    We are a group of NYU Students with different backgrounds. Our team includes people from Computer Science, Civil Engineering, Construction Management, Robotics, and Design. Our team started taking shape in April, 2017. We began with four people, and have grown to about fifteen now. Each one of us has a vision for our product and believes that we will be very successful after launching it in the market.

  • Our Vision

    To bring about such ease in real estate deals, in buying and selling of properties that it does not even require you to leave the comfort of your home. We aim to offer you a technological solution which will change the way you think about real estate entirely. We will decrease the time spent contemplating such decisions, by offering you such an absolutely immersive experience.

  • Technology

    Property models built on Revit, transported over to the Unity Game Engine, our product will feature state-of-the-art graphics and interactions, almost as if you're in a first-person game.
